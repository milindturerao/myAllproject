About

Ajay Patil & Associates, Chartered Accountants, is one of the foremost Chartered Accountant Firm consists of Professionally Qualified person who have superior quality experience in different fields. The firm is registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India in the year 2011, founded by the CA. Ajay Mahadev Patil. Our team is committed to providing you with timely and responsive service. Our firm's mission statement is “Service with Trust & Simplicity for Turning Vision into Value” The activities of the firm are headed by CA. Ajay Mahadev Patil. He has working experience of more than 10 years out of which over 7 years with top auditing firm in Navi Mumbai. He has vast experience in the field of Internal & Statutory Audit, Financial Due Diligence, Compliance with Direct & Indirect Tax, Assessment and Accounts Finalization.
